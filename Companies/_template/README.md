# Company Research Template

For each company you're interviewing with, create a folder under `Companies/[CompanyName]/`.

All company folders (except this template) are gitignored — your research and prep docs will never be committed.

---

## Recommended Files to Add

### Job Description
Save the full job description as a markdown or text file.
`Job Listing - [Role Title].md`

### General Company Research
Notes on the company: product, business model, market position, AI strategy, competitive landscape, recent news.
`[Company] - General Information.md`

### Deep Research
More detailed research on the product area you'd own, the team structure, and strategic priorities.
`[Company] - Deep Research.md`

### Recruiter Guidance
Any specific guidance from the recruiter about interview format, focus areas, or what each interviewer cares about. This is high-value — save it verbatim.
`What to Expect - [Interview Type].md`

### Interview Prep Docs (generated by skills)
Skills will generate and save prep docs here automatically.
`Prep - [Interview Type].md`

---

## Tips

- The more context you add, the more tailored your prep materials will be
- Include recruiter guidance verbatim — specific framing from the interviewer matters
- Add Glassdoor/Blind interview reviews if you find them
- Note the names and backgrounds of your interviewers if known
